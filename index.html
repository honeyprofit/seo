about me

Hi There, 
I'm a product designer and front-end web developer
with 3+ years of experience in web design.
 After graduating from Business school with a BA and Art school with an MA, I chose to follow my passion for digital design and programming. To sharpen my skills, I enrolled in UI/UX and coding bootcamps, where I discovered my favorite part of programming: problem-solving. I love the challenge of finding systematic and logical solutions to bring designs to life as functional websites.
 I'm currently expanding my skillset with React and Next.js, always eager to learn new technologies and stay ahead of the curve. I'm seeking opportunities where I can serve as a bridge between design and technology, leveraging my unique blend of creative design thinking and technical expertise to craft seamless digital experiences.
 When I'm not immersed in code or design, you'll find me at the piano or tending to my growing collection of plants. These moments in nature not only help me recharge but often provide fresh perspectives and inspiration for my design work.
