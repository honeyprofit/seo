about me

Hi There, 
I’m a Global Marketing Strategist with a decade of experience helping businesses thrive in international markets.
My passion lies in elevating Korean brands on the global stage, connecting their unique value to audiences worldwide.
My journey began in international marketing and business development, working across diverse markets from Europe to the Americas, Australia, and Southeast Asia. Through countless client interactions, I’ve learned that the key to successful marketing isn’t just selling products—it’s understanding customers’ stories and turning those insights into impactful strategies.
During the pandemic, I witnessed the rapid digital transformation of traditionally offline markets, particularly in the UK. This experience underscored the critical role of SEO, keyword strategies, and mastering search engine algorithms in today’s e-commerce landscape. It became clear that digital marketing isn’t just an add-on—it’s the backbone of modern global business.
For me, marketing isn’t just a job—it’s about building bridges and creating opportunities. My goal is to help Korean businesses not just participate in global markets but define their direction and lead the way.
